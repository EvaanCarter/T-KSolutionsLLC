About Us

Welcome to T & K Solutions LLC a proud, family-owned and veteran-owned small business specializing in decks, roofs, and all your home improvement needs. With a close-knit team of around ten dedicated professionals, we bring a personal touch and strong values to every project we take on. Our experience combines the discipline of military service with the warmth and integrity of a family operation, ensuring every deck, roof, or renovation is completed with attention to detail and the highest level of care.

Being a small business allows us to offer unmatched personalization and responsiveness to our clients. At T & K Solutions LLC, you’re not just another customer—you’re a neighbor and part of our extended community. We take the time to understand your vision and goals, working side by side with you to create a space you’re proud of. Whether you need expert roof repairs, a custom deck, or general construction services, you can trust our skilled, reliable team to deliver lasting results and a smooth, stress-free experience from start to finish.
